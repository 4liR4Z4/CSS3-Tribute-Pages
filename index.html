Son Goku

Born Kakarot (カカロット Kakarotto), is a Saiyan raised on Earth and the overall main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. Kakarot originally was sent to destroy Earth as an infant, there he would be adopted by Grandpa Gohan who named him Son Goku. Overtime, he became the strongest warrior in the universe.
